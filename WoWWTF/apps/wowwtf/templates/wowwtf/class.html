Replacements

A decent replacement for the Rot-Scour Ring ring is the Band of the Ancient Dredger from the Siege of Boralus dungeon.

If you don't want to spend gold or cannot craft a Darkmoon Deck: Fathoms the best option to replace it is the Kul Tiran Cannonball Runner from Tol Dagor.

Unfortunately for azerite traits most of the raid traits are the highest dps traits so there are only 4 chances at them per week, but the priority weekly bosses are:

  • Mythrax
  • Fetid Devourer
